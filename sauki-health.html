Sauki Health

Primary Healthcare Outreach

We work with our partners and volunteers to provide a community evidence-based and high-quality healthcare to underserved African communities. Our goal is to improve the access African rural women and children have to superior health and as a result promote wellness in Africa’s rural communities.

Volunteer

Maternal Healthcare

Our commitment includes the health care dimensions of family planning, preconception, prenatal, and postnatal care in order to ensure a positive and fulfilling experience. We support rural African women with facilities and personnel to help reduce maternal and infant mortality rate in the Country.

Volunteer

School-Based Mobile Clinic

The goal is to detect early and improve. It unlocks the power of preventative healthcare and early detection as well as promote student success and community wellness. It is available to students, each with parental consent, and available to the school’s teaching and non-teaching staff.
